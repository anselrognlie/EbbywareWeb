about

At Ebbyware Software, we know that technology is all around us. Media streaming, social media, gaming, always-connected devices — it's everywhere!

Even though we're spoiled for choice, sometimes you still just can't find what you're looking for. That one specific utility. The developers at Ebbyware Software know how that feels, and when we run into that situation ourselves, we want to fix it! And if it helps someone else as a result, then so much the better.

Our software catalog is somewhat eclectic, as it is driven by personal need rather than a single overarching goal. We just just like writing software, and we hope that some of it is helpful to you! Our software is open source, so even if our needs don't match yours exactly, you can always take a fork and craft the perfect solution for your own use.
