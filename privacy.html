privacy policy

Last updated: 20 Dec 2019

We collect no user data from our apps. Data entered into any of our apps is kept local to that app and not transmitted back to Ebbyware Software at any time.

The sole possible exception is if you have elected to share diagnostic information, which can help developers improve apps by providing crash data for analysis. In that case, personal information present in memory at the time of the crash may be exposed to the developers. We pledge to use such data only for debugging purposes, and to purge any and all such data from our systems as rapidly as possible.

Additionally, our web site also collects no user data, and stores no information in cookies or any other persistent storage.

Source code access and feature or support requests are handled through GitHub. As such, please review their privacy policy before making use of either of those services.

Direct email communication is handled through Gmail, and any emails sent to the Ebbyware Software contact address may be retained on Google servers until such time as Ebbyware Software contributors are able to review them. Please review Google's privacy policies before contacting us. Additionally, emails may continue to be retained for further support purposes. Any personal information included in such emails shall be kept confidential to the greatest extent possible under applicable laws. Use of the Ebbyware Software contact address constitutes your acceptance of these terms and conditions.
